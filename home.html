About us

Welcome To Our Website

Jenkinson's Aquarium is renowned for its diverse array of marine life, featuring exhibits that showcase everything from tropical fish and sharks to penguins and seals. The aquarium places a strong emphasis on conservation and education, offering interactive experiences and educational programs designed to foster an appreciation for marine ecosystems and wildlife conservation efforts.

Services

Jenkinson's SeaLife offers a range of services aimed at providing unforgettable marine experiences. From educational tours for school groups to entertaining birthday parties surrounded by fascinating sea creatures, they cater to diverse interests and age groups. With expert staff providing informative talks and interactive demonstrations, visitors can learn about marine life conservation while enjoying up-close encounters with various species. Whether it's a family outing or a school field trip, Jenkinson's SeaLife ensures a memorable and educational experience for all.

Aquarium Exhibit

Jenkinson's Sealife Aquarium features a wide range of marine life exhibits,including sharks, penguins and a variety of fish species.Visitors can explore these exhibits and learn about thr fascinating underwater world..

Educational Programs

The aquarium offers educational programs for visitors of all ages, including guide tours, interactive feeding sessions, educational presentations. These programs aim to teach visitors about marine conservation, animal behaviour, and the importance of preserving ocean ecosystems.

Events & Parties

Jenkinson's aquarium hosts events and parties, inclusing birthday parties, corporate events, and special themed nights. These events offer a unique opportunity for visitors to enjoy the aquarium's exhibits in a fun and festive atmosphere.
