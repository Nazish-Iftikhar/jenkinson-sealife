LEARNING EXPERIENCE

Amazing learning experience For everyone

Our aquariums are a great place to learn more about our oceans, and the incredible creatures that live in them.

Whatever your age, we’ve got a fun and inspiring experience for you:

1) Expert daily talks to teach you all about our creatures
2) Self-guided tours and workshops for schools at most of our aquariums
3) Multimedia experiences like Shark Mission and Art Aquarium to help you dive deeper into our creatures’ lives
4) Behind the Scenes tours at some of our aquariums, hosted by our experts and helping you uncover our creatures’ secrets
5) Annual events like Shark Week to teach you about marine conservation
